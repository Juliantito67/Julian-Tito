Creator

Toru Iwatani is a Japanese video game designer best known as the creator of Pac-Man. He created the game in 1980 to make something fun and accessible, unlike violent arcade games.

Pac-Man

Pac-Man is a yellow circular character who eats dots in a maze while avoiding ghosts. The player controls his movement to survive and score points.

Ghosts

The ghosts (Blinky, Pinky, Inky, Clyde) chase Pac-Man. Each has a unique behavior. Power pellets allow Pac-Man to eat them temporarily.
