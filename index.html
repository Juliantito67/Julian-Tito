HOW IT ALL STARTED CHARACTERS

TORU IWATANI IS A JAPANESE GAME DESIGNER WHO CREATED PAC-MAN IN 1980. HE WANTED A NON-VIOLENT, FUN GAME FOR EVERYONE, DIFFERENT FROM SHOOTERS.

PAC-MAN

A YELLOW CHARACTER THAT EATS DOTS THROUGH A MAZE. HE MUST AVOID GHOSTS WHILE COLLECTING POINTS.

GHOSTS

BLINKY PINKY INKY CLYDE

THEY CHASE PAC-MAN. POWER PELLETS TURN THEM BLUE SO THEY CAN BE EATEN.
