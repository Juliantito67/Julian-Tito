GAME PLANS
PAC-MAN is a yellow, circle-shaped character with a mouth that opens and closes as he moves. He travels through a maze eating dots while avoiding ghosts.
MAZE STATS
240 dots - 4 Power Pellets - Fruit bonuses
GHOST RULES
Power Pellet makes ghosts BLUE - edible.
HOW IT ALL STARTED
Toru Iwatani joined Namco in late 1970s, wanted a non-violent game. Pac-Man released 1980 - maze chase icon.
ARCADE TIPS
- Use tunnels to warp.
- Ghost AI: Blinky chases, Pinky ambushes, Inky flanks, Clyde roams.

TORU IWATANI
BORN: JAN 25, 1955 | ALIVE AND LEGENDARY
Japanese game designer, creator of Pac-Man (1980). Designed to move away from violent shooters. Philosophy: a game about eating, not killing.
