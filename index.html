🎮 HOW IT ALL STARTED

TORU IWATANI — Japanese video game designer, creator of PAC-MAN. In the late 1970s, while at NAMCO, he rejected violent "shooting" games and aimed to create something friendly, accessible, and universal.

That vision became PAC-MAN (1980) — a maze-chase where players guide a yellow hero eating dots, outsmarting ghosts. No guns, no war, just pure arcade charm. A revolution!

🕹️ "Eating dots and running from ghosts — fun for everyone" — Iwatani
🟡 GAME PLANS · PAC-MAN

PAC-MAN is a yellow, circle-shaped character with a chomping mouth. He travels through a blue maze, gobbling white pellets while avoiding four unique ghosts. The player controls direction — always in motion, strategic turns, and survival instincts.
